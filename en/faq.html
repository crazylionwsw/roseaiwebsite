Frequently Asked Questions

Everything restaurant owners ask about RoseAI

A clear guide to phone forwarding, multilingual answering, POS integrations, privacy, setup, pricing and human handoff before you start a trial.

Book a demo Back to home
01Do I need to change my restaurant phone number?
No. RoseAI connects through call forwarding, so your Google Business Profile, menus, storefront signage and existing phone number can stay exactly as they are. Basic setup usually takes about 10 minutes.
02Which languages does RoseAI support?
RoseAI supports English, Mandarin, Cantonese and Spanish, and can automatically detect the caller language. It is designed for multilingual restaurants across Greater Vancouver.
03Can it connect with Toast, Square, Lightspeed or TouchBistro?
Yes. RoseAI can connect with major POS systems and send phone orders into your workflow. For older systems without an open API, SMS, email or kitchen-ticket printing can be used as fallback options.
04What happens if the AI is unsure or mishears something?
RoseAI repeats key order details for confirmation, flags uncertain items for staff review, and can transfer to a human for allergies, complaints, complex customizations or high-value orders.
05Will customers dislike speaking with AI?
Most guests care most about getting answered quickly, being understood and placing the order correctly. You can choose whether the greeting clearly discloses the AI assistant, and callers can still request a human.
06Where is data stored, and is this privacy compliant?
RoseAI is designed with Canadian privacy expectations in mind, with call and order data prioritized for Canadian storage regions plus export, deletion and access-control workflows. For obligations specific to your business, confirm with your legal advisor.
07How long does setup take, and do I need an IT team?
Most restaurants can go live within 7 days. You do not need an internal IT team: we help configure your menu, hours, FAQs, language preferences, human handoff rules and POS or printer workflow.
08Can RoseAI still answer if the restaurant internet goes down?
Yes. RoseAI runs in the cloud, so calls can still be answered and logged when your in-store network has issues. Local printing depends on your hardware setup, and 4G printer fallback can be configured when needed.
09How does pricing and contract length work?
Plans are monthly, with final pricing based on your quote. Most restaurants can start with a lightweight plan and upgrade based on call volume, number of locations, POS integrations and support requirements.
10Can I try it before deciding?
Yes. You can book a demo or request a trial using your real menu and real call scenarios before deciding whether to go live.
