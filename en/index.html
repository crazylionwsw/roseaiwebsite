Each call is billed by rounded-up minute from AI pickup to hangup. An average call lasts about 2 minutes. Lite's 200 minutes ≈ 50 calls, Pro's 400 ≈ 200 calls, Elite's 1,000 ≈ 500 calls.

After included minutes, Lite charges $0.90/min, Pro $0.80/min, Elite $0.70/min, Enterprise $0.60/min. You get auto-SMS alerts at 80% / 100% / 120% usage — fully transparent. You can also set a hard cap that switches to voicemail mode automatically.
All plans have no concurrency limit — RoseAI uses cloud elastic answering, with peak-time calls dynamically distributed across our infrastructure. "Cloud elastic answering — never busy" is our default promise across every plan.

So even if 5 or 10 calls hit at once on a Friday 7pm, each customer gets answered by AI in 0 seconds — no busy signal.
RoseAI uses the latest generation of neural voice — sounds nearly human. Most customers don't realize they're talking to AI until after the call. Three-layer safety net: (1) AI repeats the order back for customer confirmation; (2) any uncertainty is tagged "needs review" and sent to the owner's phone; (3) complex situations (complaints, custom large orders) auto-route to a phone number you designate.
