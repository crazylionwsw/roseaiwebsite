FAQ

The 10 things owners always ask first.

Do I need to change my phone number?
No. We use call forwarding on your existing line, so your number on Google, your menus, and your storefront stays the same. Setup takes about 10 minutes.
Will customers know they're talking to an AI?
RoseAI uses the latest generation of neural voice models — most customers don't notice. You can also choose to disclose it ("Hi, I'm the AI assistant at Maple & Vine"). Pilot data shows customers actually rate AI calls higher: no hold music, no "please call back," no accent friction.
What if the AI mishears an order?
Three layers of safety: (1) RoseAI repeats the order back for confirmation, (2) anything uncertain is flagged for owner review on your phone, and (3) complex cases auto-route to your mobile. We back this with an under-1% error guarantee and reimburse confirmed mis-orders.
How long is the contract? Can I cancel?
All plans are month-to-month with no minimum commitment. Cancel any time from your dashboard — billing stops the following month. Annual plans get 15% off and we'll prorate the refund on unused months.
Where is my data stored? Is it compliant?
All recordings and order data are stored in Canada (AWS ca-central-1, Toronto) and fully comply with PIPEDA and BC's Personal Information Protection Act. Recordings are kept 90 days, exportable or deletable any time. We never use your data to train third-party models.
What if the internet or power goes down at the restaurant?
RoseAI runs in the cloud, completely independent of your in-store network. Even during a power outage, calls are still answered and orders are queued — they'll print as soon as you're back online. Elite and Enterprise plans include a 4G-backup printer.
Which POS systems do you support?
We integrate natively with Square, Toast, Clover, and Lightspeed. For less common systems we can build a custom integration in 5–10 business days (included with Elite/Enterprise). If you'd rather skip POS integration entirely, we can deliver orders by SMS or print directly to a kitchen ticket printer.
Are prices in CAD? Any hidden fees?
All prices are in CAD, plus GST/PST. There are no setup fees, no hardware deposits, no activation charges. If you need a kitchen ticket printer, hardware runs about CAD $180 (one-time, optional — you can also use your own).
How does the 1-month free trial work?
No credit card, no deposit required. You get full Pro-plan functionality for 14 days — multilingual answering, POS sync, the works. We'll reach out 48 hours before the trial ends. Continuing is entirely your call.

Call 778-325-4966, email hello@roseai.ca, or message us on WhatsApp. We have local customer support in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese — based here in Greater Vancouver.
